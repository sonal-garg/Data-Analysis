Introduction¶

I have selected the movie data set from TMDB for my exploration. This dataset contains information of 10,866 movies released between 1960 to 2015 on 21 parameters.The dataset gives me an opportunity to analyse the movie watching patterns, the cast, the revenure generated from different genres of movie.

There are a number of questions that come to mind after a quick glance through the dataset. Some of them are as follows:

- What are the genre of movies that have been most hit?
- Is there any relation between budget and revenue generated?
- Which is the most profitable and the least profitable movie of all time?
- Which actor/actress has done most number ofsuccessful movies?
- Who is the most successful director?
- Which is the most succesful production company?
- Which has been the most succesful year so far?
- How are runtimes of movies changing over the years? Is there any corelation between runtime and popularity?
- What are the popularity trends over the years?
- What are the revenue trends over the years?
- What makes the movie successful?

Lets analyse the dataset to get the answer to these and more questions.

Conclusions¶

After analysing various parameters of the movie data, I have come up with the following conclusion:

Following parameters ensure for a movie to be succesful:

  • Genre: Genre of the movie should be either Drama or Comedy, preferrably Drama.
  • Successful cast : Following are the top 3 most succesful cast members: Robert De Niro,Samuel L. Jackson,Tom Hanks.
  • Smaller runtime: Runtime of the movie should be within 100 minutes.
  • Good production house : Universal Pictures have produced maximum number of hits.
  • Great Director: Top 3 most succeful directors are: Clint Eastwood,Woody Allen,Steven Spielberg.
  • Appropriate time of release : Months June and May have recorded the maximum profit over the years.

Following are the trends observed over the years:

  • Avatar is most profitable movie.
  • The Warrior's Way and Hubble 3D are the least profitable movies.
  • 2010 has been the least profitable year so far followed by 2006.
  • 2013 has been the most successful year so far.
  • September followed by october has seen the most number of releases over the years.
  • Hubble 3D and The Warrior's Way are the movies made on maximum budget. They both released in 2010
  • So, Avatar has been the most revenue generating movie. It released in 2009.Star Wars: The Force Awakens came close in 2015 but did not surpass the record
  • Jurassic park is the most popular movie of all time.
  • The Hospital is the least popular movie.
  • The average budget of a succesful movie is around 23.4 million with the average revenue around 87.29 million.
  • Movies made on higher budgets also produce greater revenues.

2.The above conclusions are not a definite way(100% solution) of a successful movie. These are some of the many parameters that could make a movie successful. However, it does show that with the above parameters in place, there is a high probabality that a movie might be successful.
